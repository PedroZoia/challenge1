Radiance

Radiance, the antagonist in Hollow Knight, is a transcendent and imposing entity. It is the manifestation of the Radiant Light and represents a divine and threatening presence. Radiance is shrouded in mystery and is considered the source of the corruption that plagues Hallownest. The confrontation with her is an epic and challenging battle, requiring skill and strategy from the player. Her power-imbued presence and stunning design make her a memorable and impactful figure in the game narrative.
